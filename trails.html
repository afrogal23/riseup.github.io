Menagesha Mt. Wochecha Hike

Suba Menagesha used to be a private park for the last emperor king Haile Selassie I. the park consists of many endemic plants

Information on Menagesh Mountain Hike
Distance 5.07 mi
Thechnical Difficulty Difficult
Maximum Elivation 11,120 ft
Minimum Elivation 9,650 ft
Trail type loop

This hike starts at the abandoned nature center near the top of Menegasha Forest Park. This loop route offers two ways to get to the peak. There mostly likely are other ways to the top. The peak, Mt. Wochecha, is the highest point in this massif. Mt. I give this a difficult rating because some areas of the train go through brush and are not clearly marked, and there is some easy, brief scrambling near the top. Also, if you start this hike from the ranger station at the entrance of Menagesha Forest Park, not the abandoned nature center, it is a long hike requiring most of the day. Each way from the Ranger Station up to the abandoned nature center is about 2.25 miles (3.6 km).

Bale Mountains national park

Information on Bale Mountain Hike
Distance 6.9 mi
Thechnical Difficulty Moderate
Maximum Elivation 13,532 ft
Minimum Elivation 12,779 ft
Trail type one way

This hike on the Bale Mountains is a one way trail and easy to walk. The hike is accomodated with accidental siteing of wild animals and beautiful endemic birds. For those who want to make it to the very top of the mountain behold for a rare phenomena in Ethiopia, SNOW.
